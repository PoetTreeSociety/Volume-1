A multimedia literary project that blends urban poetry with an interactive digital experience. Through raw and rhythmic verses, the text explores themes of childhood trauma, systemic struggle, and the search for spiritual redemption. The collection utilizes a metaphorical notebook aesthetic, featuring analytical "teacher's notes" and 3D elements to deepen the reader's engagement with the narratives. Each poem serves as a cinematic vignette, capturing intense personal stories ranging from the consequences of addiction to the complexities of identity. Ultimately, the work functions as a soulful dialogue between the author’s past and the reader’s own creative potential.
